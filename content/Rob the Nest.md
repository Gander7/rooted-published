## Overview
A high-energy free-for-all where teams sprint to grab objects from the centre pile and steal from other teams' corners.

## Setup
1. Place a large pile of objects (balls, bean bags, pool noodles, etc.) in the centre of the gym.
2. Divide players into 3-4 teams.
3. Assign each team a corner of the gym as their "nest."
4. All players start in their corner.

## How to Play
1. On "GO," players sprint to the centre pile and grab **one object at a time**.
2. Carry it back to your team's corner (nest) and place it down.
3. Once the centre pile is empty, players can steal objects from **other teams' nests** -- still only one at a time.
4. Players cannot guard their nest or block others from taking objects.
5. When the leader blows a whistle (or after a set time), everyone freezes.

## How to Win
The team with the most objects in their nest when the whistle blows wins the round.

## Variations
- Allow guarding with one designated "guard" per team who cannot leave the nest area. The guard can tag anyone who enters the nest -- tagged players must drop the object and return to their own corner empty-handed before trying again.
- Play with a time limit (e.g., 2 minutes per round) instead of waiting for the action to die down.
- Add a rule that you cannot steal from the same team twice in a row.

## Leader Notes
- Use soft, easy-to-carry objects. Avoid anything that could hurt if dropped or thrown.
- The "one object at a time" rule is critical -- enforce it strictly or teams will grab armfuls.
- Rounds go fast. Plan for 2-3 rounds and keep score across rounds.
- This game gets chaotic quickly, which is part of the fun. Set clear boundaries so players are not running into walls.
