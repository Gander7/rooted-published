## Overview
Everyone holds hands in a circle around a garbage can and tries to pull others into it -- touch the can or break grip and you're out.

## Setup
- Place a garbage can in the center of an open area.
- Have all players form a circle around the garbage can and hold hands.

## How to Play
- On "Go," players pull, push, and maneuver while holding hands to try to force other players into the garbage can.
- If you touch the garbage can, you are out.
- If you let go of someone's hand (break grip), you are out.
- There are no other rules.
- As players are eliminated, the circle shrinks and gets more intense.

## How to Win
The last player standing who has not touched the can or broken grip wins.

## Variations
- Use a smaller object in the center to make it harder to avoid.
- Play with multiple cans for a larger group.

## Leader Notes
- Use a lightweight plastic garbage can -- not a heavy metal one. Safety first.
- Keep an eye out for overly aggressive pulling that could lead to injuries.
- This game escalates fast. Remind players to have fun but not to send anyone to the hospital.
- Works best with groups of similar size and strength, or mix it up intentionally for chaos.
