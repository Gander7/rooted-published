## Overview
An elimination game played in an enclosed pit where players hit a ball with an open hand to knock it into opponents below the knee.

## Setup
1. Set up a gaga pit. If you do not have one, arrange chairs, benches, or other barriers in a circle or octagon to form a boundary. Or just use a room.
2. All players start inside the pit with one hand touching the wall.
3. Have a soft ball (dodgeball or foam ball) ready.

## How to Play
1. Toss the ball into the center of the pit. It bounces three times while everyone chants "Ga-Ga-Ball!" (one word per bounce).
2. After the third bounce, the ball is live.
3. Players hit the ball with their hand, aiming to knock it into other players at or below the knee.
4. If the ball touches a player at or below the knee, that player is out and must leave the pit.
5. No catching, carrying, or throwing the ball -- only open-hand hitting is allowed.
6. No double touches: after hitting the ball, a player cannot hit it again until it has touched the wall or another player.
7. Players can jump or dodge to avoid the ball.
8. If the ball goes out of the pit, the last person who touched it is out.

## How to Win
The last player standing in the pit wins the round.

## Variations
- Allow eliminated players to re-enter if the person who got them out is later eliminated.
- Play with multiple balls at once for chaos.
- Set a time limit and count how many players remain at the end.
- If people are hitting too hard, enforce an open-hand rule to prevent punching the ball.

## Leader Notes
- If you do not have a proper gaga pit, a circle of chairs works. Just make sure they are stable.
- Games can go quickly with large groups. Run multiple rounds and maybe keep a leaderboard.
