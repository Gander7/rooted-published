## Overview
A high-energy dodgeball game where one player is the President, one is the Bodyguard, and everyone else tries to take them out.

## Setup
- Have everyone form a large circle.
- Choose one person to be the President and one to be the Bodyguard.
- The President and Bodyguard stand in the center of the circle.
- Give the dodgeball to someone in the circle.

## How to Play
- The circle players try to hit the President with the dodgeball.
- The Bodyguard tries to block, deflect, or intercept the ball to protect the President.
- The President tries to dodge and avoid getting hit.
- Circle players can pass the ball around the circle to find a better angle.
- When the President is hit:
  1. The President returns to the circle.
  2. The Bodyguard becomes the new President.
  3. Whoever hit the President becomes the new Bodyguard.

## How to Win
There is no final winner -- the goal is to become the President and survive as long as possible. The game cycles continuously.

## Variations
None yet.

## Leader Notes
- Keep the circle tight enough that the President and Bodyguard can't just run endlessly but large enough to encourage cooperation in passing the ball around the circle
- If a Bodyguard is lasting too long, shrink the circle.
