## Overview
Students sprint across the room to grab a shrinking number of chairs in this high-energy elimination game. Also known as "Storm Surge."

## Setup
- Set up two rows of chairs facing each other with a gap in the middle.
- Each side should have enough chairs for half the group.
- Split students evenly between the two sides and have them sit down.

## How to Play
- On the signal, all students stand up and sprint to the opposite side to sit in a chair.
- After each round, remove 1-4 chairs from each side (remove more chairs for larger groups to keep the pace up).
- Any student left standing after a round is eliminated and sits on the floor to watch.
- Continue removing chairs and running rounds until very few players remain.

## How to Win
The last student to secure a seat on each side wins. Alternatively, when two players remain on one side, set up two chairs on one side and have a final sprint race to determine the winner.

## Variations
- Have students start in different positions each round (standing, kneeling, facing away).
- Remove chairs unevenly from each side to create chaos.
- Add obstacles in the middle that players have to navigate around.

## Leader Notes
- Watch for collisions -- students will be sprinting toward each other. Make sure the gap between sides is wide enough.
- Remove enough chairs each round to keep the game moving. If you only remove one at a time with a big group, the game drags.
- Have eliminated students cheer from the sidelines to keep energy up.
